The Woman Behind the Pins

Our Story

Born in LA. Built from love.

EST. 2018 · Los Angeles

Hi, I'm Steph G. 👋

Founder

I've been a Dodgers fan my whole life — born and raised right here in Los Angeles. Growing up watching games, repping LA pride, I always wanted a way to carry that love with me everywhere I went.

In 2018, this brand was born. What started as a passion project for custom enamel pins turned into something way bigger than I ever imagined — a community of Dodger fans, LA natives, and pin collectors who all just get it.

Every pin I design tells a story. From Shohei and Mookie to the lowriders and palm trees — it's all LA, all day. I put my heart into every piece and I'm grateful for every single person who rocks them.

Rulebook

Frequently Asked

The umpire's call on everything you need to know.

How long until my order ships?

Orders ship within 2-3 business days. LA locals get free pickup — DM us on Instagram to coordinate. We also offer stadium pickup on game days!

Are these officially licensed MLB products?

Our designs are inspired by Dodgers culture and the LA baseball community. They're fan-made, creative crossovers — not official MLB merchandise. We celebrate the culture, we don't copy it.

How do raffles work?

Enter by paying $5 per entry. The more entries, the better your odds. When the timer hits zero, we announce the winner live on Instagram. Simple as a bases-clearing double.

What payment methods do you accept?

Venmo (@Stephie33), Zelle (gstephanie3@yahoo.com), and Stripe for card payments. Whatever's easiest for you — we're flexible like a utility infielder.

Do you do custom designs?

Absolutely. If you've got an idea for a pin, sticker, or patch, follow us on Instagram and send us a message. We love creative collabs — especially if they're LA-rooted.

What if my pin arrives damaged?

That shouldn't happen, but if it does, reach out within 7 days and we'll send a replacement. No questions asked — we treat our customers better than the home plate ump.

Can I return or exchange?

Pins and patches are final sale. Shirts can be exchanged within 14 days if unworn. We're a small operation, not a big-box retailer — appreciate your understanding.

Where can I follow you?

Instagram is our home base — follow @pinthiz.la for drops, raffles, pop-up announcements, and behind-the-scenes content. That's where the action happens.
